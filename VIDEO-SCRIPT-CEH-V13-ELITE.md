# CEH Elite V13 - 60 Second Video Script
## "What Makes Version 13 Elite Different?"

**Purpose**: Hero section video for high-ticket conversion landing page  
**Duration**: 60 seconds  
**Target**: Enterprise professionals & senior IT leaders ($5K-$10K program)  
**Tone**: Authority, exclusivity, results-focused

---

## VIDEO STRUCTURE

### Scene 1: THE PROBLEM (0-8 seconds)
**Visual**: 
- Dark screen with glitching red "ACCESS DENIED" text
- Code snippets scrolling rapidly
- Frustrated professional looking at certification on wall vs. terminal screen

**Voiceover**:
*"You passed CEH. But when it's time to deliver—you freeze."*

**Text Overlay**: 
- "Theory ≠ Real-World Execution"

**Music**: Tense, dark ambient

---

### Scene 2: THE GAP (8-15 seconds)
**Visual**:
- Split screen: Left side shows "Regular CEH Course" with generic slides
- Right side shows "CEH Elite V13" with live terminal, actual exploits, red team dashboards

**Voiceover**:
*"Version 13 introduced 43 new attack vectors. Most courses teach you TO PASS. We teach you TO PERFORM."*

**Text Overlay**:
- "43 New V13 Attack Vectors"
- "Live Red Team Scenarios"

**Music**: Build tension

---

### Scene 3: THE ELITE DIFFERENCE (15-30 seconds)
**Visual**:
- Quick cuts showing:
  1. Real penetration test dashboard
  2. Student performing live exploit in iLabs
  3. EC-Council Global Instructor Award certificate (yours)
  4. Student getting job offer email
  5. Government training facility

**Voiceover**:
*"This isn't a course. It's an immersion. 12 weeks. Live attacks. Real infrastructure. Government-grade training led by EC-Council's Global Instructor of the Year. You don't just learn Version 13—you master it like a red teamer."*

**Text Overlay** (rapid succession):
- "12-Week Live Immersion"
- "Real Attack Infrastructure"
- "EC-Council Global Instructor 2022"
- "Application-Only (20 Slots)"

**Music**: Epic build, cinematic

---

### Scene 4: THE TRANSFORMATION (30-45 seconds)
**Visual**:
- Professional confidently presenting security findings to executives
- Terminal showing successful exploit chain
- Before/After salary comparison: "$65K SOC Analyst" → "$110K Penetration Testing Lead"
- Student testimonial overlay (if available, otherwise use quote card)

**Voiceover**:
*"Within 90 days, our students go from 'certified' to CONFIDENT. They're leading penetration tests. Commanding six-figure salaries. And speaking with authority clients actually trust."*

**Text Overlay**:
- "95% Pass Rate"
- "Average $40K Salary Increase"
- "Career Transformation, Not Just Certification"

**Music**: Triumphant, inspiring

---

### Scene 5: THE CALL TO ACTION (45-60 seconds)
**Visual**:
- Countdown timer showing days until application deadline
- "20 SLOTS ONLY" with slots filling up animation
- Your professional photo with credentials appearing
- Application form preview
- Calendly booking interface

**Voiceover**:
*"February 10th cohort. 20 slots. Application and interview required. If you're ready to go from theory to threat actor-level execution—apply now. Deadline: January 25th."*

**Text Overlay**:
- "Only 20 Slots Available"
- "Application Deadline: January 25, 2026"
- "Cohort Starts: February 10, 2026"
- **BUTTON**: "Apply Now" (clickable if embedded on site)

**Music**: Urgent but inspiring close
